The 4 paintings are the main illustrations so far around a tribute to a wonderful poem “The Greedy Vacuum Cleaner” written by Linda Grey-Wilson. A story of an untidy son and the mother’s plight.

The painting were all created on 6.5x4 feet boards using Pastels and each painting has 2 scenes.

Charlie in his element

Charlie is such a nice guy but when it comes to being tidy things go wrong

Clean up your act

Charlie searches for a way to clean up his act and goes to a local store. Once he gets going things get out of control and the vacuum comes alive!

Escape maybe - Shock!

Charlie tries to escape the greedy vacuum cleaner but did he make it? Charlies Mothers comes into a very clean but empty room!

“Charlie’s Mum looking for a Job”

The poem explains how Charlie's mum ends up in the Vacuum shop!
